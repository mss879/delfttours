•	Duration: 3 days / 2 nights
•	Tour Type: Private escorted tour
•	Suitable For: Short stays, add ons, couples, families, transit guests
•	Meal Plan: Breakfast included

Itinerary
Day 1 – Arrival / Kandy – The Cultural Heart
- Meet & greet at Bandaranaike International Airport
- Scenic drive through lush hill country to Kandy (approx. 3 hrs)
- En-route stops at Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage (optional) or local spice gardens
- Afternoon city tour of Kandy:
  - Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (UNESCO)
  - Walk around Kandy Lake and explore the Bazaar and Gem Museum
- Evening: enjoy a Traditional Cultural Dance Show
- Overnight in Kandy (Standard – Senani Hotel 4★ / Deluxe – Oak Ray Regency 5★)

Day 2 – Kandy → Colombo – The Vibrant Coastal Capital
- Breakfast at hotel and morning visit to Royal Botanical Gardens – Peradeniya
- Stop at Giragama Tea Factory for a tea experience and tasting
- Depart for Colombo (approx. 3 hrs)
- Afternoon city tour:
  - Galle Face Green Promenade
  - Gangaramaya Temple and Viharamahadevi Park
  - Independence Square, Red Mosque, and Lotus Tower view
- Leisure or shopping at Colombo City Centre / One Galle Face Mall
- Overnight in Colombo (Standard – Cinnamon Red 4★ / Deluxe – Cinnamon Grand 5★)

Day 3 – Departure
- Breakfast and leisure until transfer to the airport for onward flight
